About Us

We are Sainthavi, Lydia, and Vanessa. We are from Drexel University and are a first year student. We came up with the idea of the Focus Timer because it had helped us with our studying process. The timer will have you study for 25 minutes and then take a break for 5 minutes. Than we also have other features like they see a calender and check the date and plan out their week. Than there is articles that they can read about studying. Than in the top we have a Budget Maker where they can plan their budgeting and a journal to journal their feeling.
